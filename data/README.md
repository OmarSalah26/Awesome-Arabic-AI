# Data and Evaluation for Arabic AI

High-performing models depend on high-quality data. This section tracks the most important corpora, benchmarks, and leaderboards that drive Arabic AI research forward.

## üèÅ Native Benchmarking

Research is shifting from **translated benchmarks** (which suffer from cultural misalignment) to **native benchmarks** created in Arabic.

- **ArabicMMLU**: Native school exams.
- **ArabLegalEval**: Specialized legal knowledge.
- **AraTrust**: Safety and hallucination checks.
